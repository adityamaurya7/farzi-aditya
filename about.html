About Our Club

The College Tech Club is a student-led community focused on innovation, coding, robotics, and emerging technologies. We aim to create a platform where students can share knowledge, build real projects, and participate in workshops and competitions.

Our mission is to help students grow by providing technical learning opportunities and hands-on experience in a friendly atmosphere. We welcome students from all branches who have a passion for learning and creating.
